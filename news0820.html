Long-term gains of CSP-PV

Interest in hybrid CSP-PV is growing as operators seek lower cost energy for longer dispatch periods. While PV plants offer lower costs for daytime power, battery costs remain too high to offer night-time PV dispatch.

Details about 150MW Aurora CSP project South Australian

Aurora, will involve a 240 m tower surrounded by more than 12,000 heliostats. The energy reflected by the heliostats onto the receiver at the top of the tower heats up molten salt which is then stored in a heavily insulated tank.
